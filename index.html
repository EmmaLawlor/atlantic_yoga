Welcome to Atlantic Yoga studio!


A modern studio in Ballyheigue with a friendly community feel and a wide variety of classes. Whether your goal is to work out, tone up or simply relax and unwind, we have a class for you!

Situated beside the sea on the breathatking Wild Atlantic Way, enjoy spectacular views each time you join us.

Embrace this lovely space and enjoy the feeling of encouragement and ease you feel from the first class. Pared back and personable, all pretention is left at the door to provide classes and Teacher Training Programmes that encourage, rather than intimidate. A very warm, welcoming environment for all!
